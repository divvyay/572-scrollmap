Here are some locations important to the Entertainment Industry.

Cannes is considered to be the global capital of cinema. The city is known for The Cannes Film Festival, an annual invitation-only film festival.

Broadway St, New York, is known for its exciting, influential, and vibrant musicals and productions.

Hollywood is a district of Los Angeles, California known for its movie and television series production. Hollywood Boulevard is a famous street in this district that includes many theatres, tourist attractions, and the Hollywood Walk of Fame.
